the good and the bad

good

  • innovative bold design
  • analog feel due to the 3D effect
  • responsive design
  • scrolling/hovering is haptic
  • animations
  • interactive menu
  • app-like feeling
  • impression of never leaving the site
  • fun to use

bad

  • scales bad on small screens
  • effects get lost on big screens
  • design mismatch on content
  • chaotic appearance
  • overuse of animation and transitions
  • slow to use
