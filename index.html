design

'I’m here to learn' (2018) is a french fold brochure, a mock-up advertisement created in the course 'Typografie 2' by Rade Matic for the exhibition 'i am here to learn'. Where a computer is able to find patterns, the given data often convey the impression of pure chaos. With the Software 'Processing' I programmed random arrangements of the title 'i'm here to learn' and stacked them on top of each other. In the final phase a further digital abstraction was performed. The intention was that the viewer can only rudimentarily grasp whats behind the pattern.
